Programmers! Designers! Nerds of all colors! Please join us for the
Tuesday Hack Night!

What is it?

The Southern Societé of Computational Libationists (est. 1718) sponsors a weekly gathering for nerds of all colors, creeds, lingiustic allegiances, and stylistic dogmas. It's an aggressively unstructured way to hack amongst peers, converse about the vagaries of this or that methodology of developmental arts, or simply to partake of delicious libations in delightful company.

When and Where?

Our soireé happens every week, on Tuesday, at 7pm! We're generously sponsored by LaunchPad, a fine co-working space located at 643 Magazine St. To gain entry, you must enter the secret knock, which is any knock on the big glass window on Capdeville St.

You'll also find a number of us either inside or outside of Capdeville, a purveyor of fine libations located just next door.

It's my first time! What can I expect?

Naught but merriment, hacking, and chatter!

There's no agenda, no presentations, no requirements, and hackers and the hacker-curious of all levels are welcome to attend.

If you're just joining us, one of our fine greeters will be happy to introduce you to like-minded wizards of analytical enginery, or non-like minded, for the more pugnacious of you. Just walk up to one of these fine fellows and be greeted, and your first libation is on us!
